What is HTML?

HTML stands for Hypertext Markup Language. It is the language of the World Wide Web!

Why is HTML important?

HTML is important because it is the essential structure of information on a website. HTML is essentially the building block to your favorite website. It may not look very pretty on its own, but with some CSS for styling and JavaScript for some functionality, the websites we know and love come to life! HTML is what makes webpages on the internet viewable.
